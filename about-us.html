InterviewofGeeks - About Us


Interview of Geeks originated from the idea that there exists a class of readers who respond better to online content and prefer to learn new skills at their own pace from the comforts of their drawing rooms.

The journey commenced with a single interview questions on JAVA in 2021 and elated by the response it generated, we worked our way to adding fresh interview questrions and tutorials to our repository which now proudly flaunts a wealth of questions and tutorials on topics ranging from programming languages to web designing to academics and much more...

Our content and resources are freely available and we prefer to keep it that way to encourage our readers acquire as many skills as they would like to. No preconditions and no impediments. Simply Easy Learning!

Mission

Our mission is to deliver Simply Easy Learning with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of technical and non-technical subjects without any preconditions and impediments.
